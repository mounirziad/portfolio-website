About Me

I’m Mounir Ziad, a game developer who loves building systems that bring worlds to life. My work sits at the intersection of technical design and creative direction — I enjoy taking ideas from concept to implementation, shaping gameplay that feels intuitive, responsive, and meaningful.

I specialize in AI programming, character systems, and modular gameplay architecture, but I also have a deep appreciation for the artistry behind games — from composing music and designing cutscenes to fine-tuning player feedback and flow. Whether working solo or in a team, I focus on creating experiences that balance precision, emotion, and playability.

What drives me most is the process itself — that moment when a mechanic clicks, a system feels alive, and the game starts to breathe on its own. Every project is a chance to learn, experiment, and refine the craft of interactive storytelling.
