Mechanisoul

Role: Lead Programmer – Character & Combat Systems

Studio: DreamStatic Studios (Internal Project)

Duration: Aug 2025 – Present

Project Highlights

  • Engineered a modular character controller supporting extensible player mechanics and animation-driven interactions
  • Developed a reusable hack-and-slash combat framework leveraging ScriptableObjects for weapons, attacks, and effects
  • Integrated animation events with hitbox activations to synchronize combat responsiveness and feedback
  • Implemented a toggleable aiming system supporting hitscan and projectile-based targeting logic
  • Created a Zelda-inspired lock-on targeting system for fluid camera and input transitions during combat
  • Designed a centralized PlayerManager component to manage health, movement, and upgrade systems
